Instructions

How to play SPOT THE DIFFERENCE; in these 4 easy steps!

  1. Hover over the rollovers to find the images. There are 3 difficulty levels to choose from!
  2. Hover your mouse over the first black screen and observe the BEFORE image for only 5 seconds
  3. Take your mouse off of the image after the 5 seconds, and hover it over the second image
  4. You will find the AFTER image, and you only have 5 seconds to guess the difference you see!

Happy playing!
